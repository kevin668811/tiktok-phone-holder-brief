A creator brief for a foldable silicone phone holder that can stand on a desk, hang beside a laptop, support a tablet, and fold flat for travel.

Primary angle Do not lead with the product name. Lead with the messy desk pain point: the phone never has a good place to sit.
Best-fit audience Desk workers, students, travelers, creators, and anyone who watches, scrolls, charges, or works beside a laptop.
Key proof points Flexible silicone body, foldable carry shape, phone and tablet support, laptop-side hanging use, and charging opening.
Content promise In the first five seconds, viewers should understand stand, hang, fold, carry, and charge.

What The Video Needs To Prove

The viewer should see this as a simple desk and travel fix, not just another phone accessory.

It fixes the phone-where-do-I-put-it problem Start with the everyday annoyance: leaning a phone on cups, keyboards, notebooks, or the laptop edge.
It is not only a desk stand Show both standing and hanging positions so the product feels more useful than a basic phone prop.
It can support more than a phone Tablet shots help raise perceived value. Keep the angle stable and the shot believable.
It folds into something easy to carry End one scene by folding it flat and dropping it into a bag, pocket, or travel pouch.

Main Creator Angle

Open with the problem, then reveal the result. The best version feels like a useful find: one small flexible strip that cleans up the phone, laptop, tablet, charging, and travel setup.

"I thought this was just a silicone strip, but it actually fixed where my phone sits on my desk."

Recommended Shot Flow

For a 20-30 second creator video, keep the scenes tactile: bend, place, hang, charge, fold, carry.

0-3s Show the phone awkwardly leaning on a cup, keyboard, or laptop. Cut fast to the stand holding it properly.
3-7s Bend the silicone body by hand and place the phone on the desk stand angle.
7-12s Hook the stand on the side of a laptop or monitor and place the phone beside the screen.
12-18s Show the tablet setup and the charging opening while the phone stays upright.
18-25s Fold it flat, drop it into a bag, then finish with a clean desk setup and product-link CTA.

Do And Do Not Say

Do say
  • Foldable silicone phone holder or stand.
  • Works as a desk stand and screen-side hanging stand.
  • Charging opening lets the phone stay upright while plugged in.
  • Useful for desk, bedside, school, work, and travel scenes.
  • Show tablet use only when the shot looks stable.
Do not say
  • Starting with packaging or a slow unboxing.
  • Absolute claims like "never falls" or "100% non-slip."
  • Medical claims such as fixing posture or neck pain.
  • Do not imply it replaces heavy-duty tablet stands.
  • Do not show unstable shots that make the product look weak.
